Who We Are

Shivansh Khanna is an award-winning filmmaker from Mumbai, India, dedicated to documenting cultural, historical, and geopolitical stories. With a deep passion for world heritage and truthful storytelling, he creates films that highlight forgotten histories, lived realities, and civilizational identities. Shivansh Khanna Films is co-founded by Shivansh Khanna and Rajan Khanna.

Mission & Vision

Our Purpose

To preserve cultural memory and truth through compelling documentary narratives that reflect India's civilizational depth while engaging with global heritage stories.

The Heritage of Kashmir

Documentary 43 Minutes

A documentary highlighting Kashmir's cultural and philosophical contributions to Indian civilization. Featuring rare monuments and architectural wonders, it showcases ancient temples and Kashmir's historic legacy.

Broadcast on: Zee News, Aastha TV, Epic TV, Epic ON, Lok Sabha TV, Docubay, Doordarshan
Winner — Chitra Bharati Film Festival 2020

The Unfinished Agenda

Documentary

A documentary examining Pakistan-occupied Jammu, Kashmir, and Ladakh — a region lost in 1947. Featuring Indian intellectuals like Tilak Devasher, Sushant Sareen, Maj. Gen. G.D. Bakshi, and Jamyang Tsering, it explores the region's geography, culture, and complex political history.

Sanskritik Jammu Kashmir

Documentary IGNCA • Ministry of Culture

Created in collaboration with IGNCA, Ministry of Culture, Govt. of India. The film documents the cultural richness of Jammu & Kashmir, emphasizing its civilizational, spiritual, and historical contributions.

Gandhara: Yesterday's Heritage of Today's Afghanistan and Pakistan

Documentary Shooting in Progress

A documentary tracing the Vedic, Buddhist, and Hindu legacy of the ancient Gandhara region. The film juxtaposes Gandhara's historical glory with its present-day context.
